Current limitation:
Governance proof validation exists as a boundary, but there is no typed governance execution policy boundary that models how an approved governance decision authorizes authority lifecycle actions. Today, governance proof material can be carried and checked, but there is no production-safe execution-policy model for governance proposals, authorization decisions, enactment windows, quorum/threshold policy, replay protection, emergency mode, or action-to-lifecycle authorization.
