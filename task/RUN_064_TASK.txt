     - otherwise 0 at startup.

     - bundle activation gate controls whether the entire bundle may load;
     - revocation activation controls whether a specific revocation entry is enforced after bundle load.
   - sequence interaction:
     - future revocation bundle may advance sequence if the bundle itself is active and valid;
     - the revocation remains pending until its own activation_height is reached.
   - operational recommendation:
     - scheduled revocations should use activation_height only when operators can safely coordinate the cutover;
     - emergency compromise revocations should usually be immediate.
